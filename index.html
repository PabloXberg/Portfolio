FIFA World Cup 2026 AI Predictor

End-to-End Data Science Project · Final Bootcamp Project 2025

A comprehensive Data Science pipeline that predicts international football match outcomes using over 150 years of historical data (49,000+ matches). The application features a full FIFA World Cup 2026 tournament simulator with all 48 teams, 104 matches, group standings, and a complete knockout bracket — plus AI-powered match analysis grounded in real statistical data.

  • Engineered 13 contextual features per match (rankings, form, H2H, venue) with zero data leakage
  • Trained & compared 4 ML/DL models: XGBoost (best ~62%), Random Forest, Gradient Boosting, Neural Network
  • Integrated a Large Language Model (LLM) via OpenRouter with multi-model fallback system
  • Built an interactive 4-page Streamlit app with custom dark theme & team crest visualization
  • Simulates the entire World Cup 2026 bracket: group stage + Round of 32 → Final
